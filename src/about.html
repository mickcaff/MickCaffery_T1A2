My Story

Hi, I’m Mick. I’m software developer currently enrolled in Coder Academy’s Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp located in Brisbane, Australia due to be completed in July, 2022. Previously, I have graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Geology from the University of Queensland and a Graduate Diploma in Economics from the University of New England.


I have worked in technical roles in the oil and gas, mining, geotechnical and agriculture industries. My career so far has taken me to far flung places across Australia and the globe. From working on floating rigs in the middle of the ocean, to helping farmers sustainably grow crops, to exploring for copper and gold deposits and to surveying ground conditions for roads and bridges.


Across all these roles, I have discovered that what I enjoy most is working on challenging problems and creating innovative solutions. Building upon my experience I’m focused on helping projects which I am passionate about solve their most challenging problems through code.

Interests

I’m interested in emerging technologies particularly in IOT. I have a great love for design and enjoy working on my 3D printers. Outside of tech, you’ll find me either mountain biking, surfing or hiking around the Sunshine Coast.
